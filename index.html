My Projects

Agricorn

A desktop-based application to determine the types of diseases on corn plant leaves. agricorn is developed with PyQt5 and deep learning YOLOv5 in python programming.

Estrunauts

Cattle estrus detector based on thermal camera and deep learning YOLOv5. Estrunauts received PKM-KC 2022 funding from Dikti and as finalist of Pekan Ilmiah Mahasiswa Nasional (PIMNAS ke-35)

Body Boundaries Mannequin

A prototype of a body boundaries teaching aid with media in the form of a mannequin equipped with an ultrasonic sensor and audio speaker as output. Collaborative project in the Tanoto Student Research Award 2022

Smart Cattle Biodigester
